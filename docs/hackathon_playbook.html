AI Health Guardian

Hackathon Playbook for learning, pitching, demoing, and answering judges with confidence.

Symptom Checker Patient Data Visualization Disease Trend Dashboard Skin Condition Classifier

1. One-Line Pitch

AI Health Guardian is a responsive healthtech web app that combines symptom guidance, patient trend visualization, local disease trend analytics, and AI-powered skin condition awareness into one easy-to-use platform for early health support.

2. What Problem We Solve

  • People often do not know how to interpret symptoms, daily vitals, or health trends.
  • Health information is scattered across different apps and tools.
  • Early warnings and simple health awareness are often missed.
  • Hackathon goal: make healthcare guidance more accessible, visual, and proactive.

3. Core Modules

Symptom Checker Tool

User selects symptoms, the app maps likely combinations, and returns guided health advice with caution text.

  • Checklist + typed symptom input
  • AI-assisted analysis
  • Safe output with medical guidance note

Patient Data Visualization

Shows BMI, sleep, steps, heart risk, and related trends in a readable dashboard.

  • Charts for local health data
  • Dashboard for abnormal trend awareness
  • Supports reports and monitoring

Disease Trend Dashboard

Uses local trend data and filters by disease, region, and time window to show spikes and hotspots.

  • Trend curve and bar chart
  • Abnormal day highlights
  • Gemini AI summary from filtered data

Skin Condition Classifier

Uploads a skin image and uses Gemini image understanding to classify possible issues and provide awareness advice.

  • Image upload / capture
  • AI image analysis
  • Awareness-oriented result text

4. Architecture to Explain

Layer What to Say
Frontend Built in Flutter with responsive layouts so the same app works on web and mobile-style screens.
State / Logic Uses provider-like shared services and BLoC in onboarding/questionnaire areas for cleaner health input handling.
AI Layer Gemini API is used for symptom reasoning, skin image understanding, medicine explanation, and disease trend summaries.
Data Patient health values are stored and visualized locally or through backend-connected health history flows.

5. Demo Flow

  1. Open dashboard and give a 20-second overview of the product vision.
  2. Show onboarding/health input and explain that we collect core vitals and health profile data.
  3. Open symptom checker and run one realistic symptom example.
  4. Open patient dashboard charts and show BMI, sleep, steps, and heart-risk trends.
  5. Open disease trend dashboard, apply filters, and generate Gemini summary.
  6. Open skin classifier and show image analysis for awareness guidance.
  7. Close with report generation / emergency support as bonus innovation features.

6. 90-Second Stage Script

Start: "We built AI Health Guardian, a healthtech platform that brings symptom guidance, patient trend tracking, disease analytics, and image-based skin awareness into one responsive app."

Middle: "Instead of showing isolated data points, our system helps users understand what those signals mean. We visualize vitals over time, detect abnormal trend patterns, and use Gemini AI to turn data into understandable health insights."

End: "Our goal is not to replace doctors, but to make early awareness, monitoring, and health information more accessible. For a hackathon prototype, this demonstrates a practical, scalable direction for preventive digital health."

7. Likely Judge Questions and Strong Answers

Question Suggested Answer
Why is this different from a chatbot? It is not only conversational. It combines structured symptom input, trend visualization, image analysis, and disease analytics in one health workflow.
Why use Gemini? Gemini gives us one flexible AI layer for text plus image understanding. That lets us support symptom guidance, skin analysis, and trend summaries with one model family.
Is this real-time? For demo purposes, we support user-entered vitals, generated local disease datasets, and AI summaries. The architecture can later connect to live clinical sources.
What is the impact? It improves early awareness, helps users understand patterns instead of raw numbers, and makes health information more visual and actionable.
How can this scale? We can add live hospital feeds, wearable sync, region-wise disease data, and personalized risk models while keeping the same dashboard and AI summarization structure.

  • Helps with early awareness and triage-style support.

Patient Data Visualization
